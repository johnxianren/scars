# Compliance requirements (excerpt)

## §4.2 Quote issuance audit

Every issuance of a quote MUST emit a complete audit trail — one record per
priced line — at the moment of issuance. This applies to repeat issuances of
an identical basket (e.g. checkout retries): each issuance is a separate
regulated event and must produce its own full trail.

Quarterly spot-audits reconcile issuance counts against audit-trail counts.
A shortfall is a reportable incident.
